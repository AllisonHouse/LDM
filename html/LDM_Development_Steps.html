LDM Development Steps


2004-04-21

  1. Decide on a subproject from the "Tasks" list.
    1. See the results under the "Surveys" tab to help you decide what subproject to work on.  If the subproject hasn't been the object of a survey, then wait until it has if it will involve some major effort.  Remember, what you think is important isn't necessary what is important.
    2. Communicate with other developers and with the project manager about your decision via the developer forum.
    3. If your desired subproject isn't on the list, then communicate with the project manager and convince them it's needed.  Use the developer forum for hashing out new subprojects.
  2. Work on the project.
    1. Follow the CVS policy described in the "LDM and CVS" document.
    2. Communicate with other developers via the developers forum.
    3. Keep the tasks updated in the subproject.
    4. Make sure your work works!  Remember to try it on multiple systems.
